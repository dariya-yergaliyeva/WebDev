Сorrect perception of information

In a world where truths can blur,
Information's path we must ensure.
To see the facts, clear and bright,
We must question all, and seek the light.

Our minds must sift, not take for granted,
What’s shown, what's said, what’s planted.
With open eyes, we must engage,
And let our wisdom guide the stage.

Perception shifts with every view,
So understand, not just pursue.
Truth is not just what you see,
But how it fits with all you believe.

So listen close and seek what's real,
For truth and knowledge, we must feel.
