Top Attractions in Bali

From stunning beaches to lush rice terraces, Bali is a destination that offers something for every traveler.

  • Visit the sacred Tanah Lot Temple, a sea temple perched on a rocky outcrop.
  • Explore the mesmerizing Ubud Monkey Forest in the heart of Bali.
  • Hike up Mount Batur for a sunrise view like no other.
  • Relax on the golden sands of Kuta Beach.
